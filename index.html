Rules

15 x 15 connected land map with lakes. Armies move one square in any of 8 directions and tanks move up to two.

Both empires begin with 3 armies and 1 armored tank. Armies have 5 HP; tanks have 10 HP.

10 cities produce a new army every 5 turns for their owner. A city changes owner when an army enters it.

Enemy armies on the same square fight randomly until one army reaches 0 of 5 hitpoints.

Win when the opponent has no units and no owned cities left. Black unoccupied cities do not need to be conquered.
